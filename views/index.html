Our Aim


Millions of individuals throughout the world do not have access to or cannot afford basic health care. Furthermore, medical staff have been in exceptionally high demand because to the COVID-19 Pandemic, with not enough to go around for everyone. We propose an AI system that can assist medical staff and potentially substitute them, so that everyone receives the care they deserve while workers can focus on saving lives.
